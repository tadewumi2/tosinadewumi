Background

I’ve had quite the journey—from studying English Literature in my undergraduate days to becoming a web developer in the making! As an international student in Canada, I’ve embraced learning and adapting to new environments, finding joy in every challenge and milestone.

My Teaching and Volunteering

With over 8 years of teaching experience, I’ve mastered the art of breaking down complex ideas—an invaluable skill in tech. I’ve volunteered at tech workshops and once served as a judge at a UNESCO-recognized spelling competition. Through these experiences, I’ve discovered my love for helping others grow, whether it’s by mentoring, creating tutorials, or building tech tools that simplify life.

Personal Interests

When I’m not coding or building web projects, I’m immersed in tech communities, attending events, and learning about the latest trends. I also enjoy creating motivational content, especially for those navigating careers in tech. Oh, and I have a creative side—I love designing with CSS animations, experimenting with gradients, and occasionally diving into storytelling on my YouTube channel.
